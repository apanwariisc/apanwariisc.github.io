Ashish Panwar

Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research India

I enjoy working on fundamental computer systems problems. In my current role at Microsoft Research India (since 2022), we explore Systems and AI Infrastructure research with a particular focus on optimizing large language model (LLM) inference. I obtained my MSc (Engg) and PhD both from CSA @ Indian Institute of Science where I worked with Prof. K. Gopinath & Prof. Arkaprava Basu.
